As a UI/UX designer at Canva, I recommend this modern, conversion-optimized color palette for your ecommerce website:

### Primary Colors
- **Primary (Teal/Cyan)**: `#00B4A6` - Trustworthy, modern, stands out
- **Primary Hover**: `#009688` - Slightly darker for interaction
- **Primary Light**: `#E0F7F5` - For backgrounds and subtle accents

### Accent Colors
- **Accent (Coral)**: `#FF6B6B` - Warm, inviting, great for CTAs
- **Accent Hover**: `#FF5252` - Deeper for hover states
- **Accent Light**: `#FFE5E5` - Soft backgrounds

### Neutral Colors
- **Background**: `#FFFFFF` - Pure white for main content
- **Surface**: `#FAFAFA` - Off-white for cards and sections
- **Gray 50**: `#F9FAFB` - Lightest gray
- **Gray 100**: `#F3F4F6` - Very light gray
- **Gray 200**: `#E5E7EB` - Borders and dividers
- **Gray 300**: `#D1D5DB` - Subtle borders
- **Gray 400**: `#9CA3AF` - Placeholder text
- **Gray 500**: `#6B7280` - Secondary text
- **Gray 600**: `#4B5563` - Body text
- **Gray 700**: `#374151` - Headings
- **Gray 800**: `#1F2937` - Dark text
- **Gray 900**: `#111827` - Darkest text

### Semantic Colors
- **Success**: `#10B981` - Green for positive actions
- **Warning**: `#F59E0B` - Amber for warnings
- **Error**: `#EF4444` - Red for errors
- **Info**: `#3B82F6` - Blue for information

### Why This Palette?

1. **Teal Primary** (#00B4A6)
   - Modern and trustworthy
   - Great for ecommerce (conveys reliability)
   - Accessible contrast ratios
   - Works well with product images

2. **Coral Accent** (#FF6B6B)
   - Warm and inviting
   - Creates urgency for CTAs
   - Complements teal beautifully
   - Eye-catching without being aggressive

3. **Clean Neutrals**
   - Professional gray scale
   - Excellent readability
   - Product images stand out
   - Timeless and versatile

### Usage Guidelines

- **Primary**: Buttons, links, brand elements, highlights
- **Accent**: CTAs, sale badges, important actions
- **Neutrals**: Backgrounds, text, borders, cards
- **Semantic**: Status messages, alerts, feedback

### Accessibility
- All color combinations meet WCAG AA standards
- High contrast for text readability
- Color-blind friendly combinations
